My Teaching Process !

Primary Phase Of A Topic !

Focus On Concepts First

I strongly believe that the best teaching always focuses on the concepts first. No matter how ignorant a student is, if he properly understands a chapter in beginning it always ends up by invoking his/her interest in the subject.

Secondary Phase

Introduce to the intermediate questions !

The students after having a proper grasp over the topic shoulld be exposed to intermediate questions so that their learning could become dynamic !

End Phase

Expertise and Advanced Questions !

The students in this phase are ready to be exposed to the tough questions and sections of the topic. This method of teaching always works logically as well as rationally and helps the students to develop a broad perspective in the subject matters.
